I am a Ph.D. candidate in division of Information Science of Engineering at KTH Royal Institute of Technology.
My research interests include probabilistic graphical models, Bayesian inference, machine learning, optimization. I also worked with stochastic models and complex networks. Applications of my research work are demonstrated via image processing, speech recognition, communication networks, social networks. My recent work is mainly on the practical inference & learning algorithm/model development within probabilistic graphical models.
